Welcome to the Crisis in Syria News Site

Thank you for visiting our website. We strive every day to make sure that the general public is informed of what is happening around the world and especially the war in Syria. This site is intended for anyone that would like to be more informed or lend a hand in helping out with the tragic cause. It is important to keep yourself up to date on the Syrian war and on what is happening with Refugees coming into the United States. We have journalists working on this site 7 days a week in order to provide the best information.
